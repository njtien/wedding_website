About Our Wedding

We are having a small, intimate wedding at a lodge built along the side of a waterfall. Our ceremony will take place out on the terrace underneath a heated tent. Dinner will be served inside. Guests are welcome to slip in and out of doors throughout the evening to enjoy the view of the falls.

If you'd like to visit the adjoining park, we recommend arriving to the wedding early. Just outside of the lodge, there is a large public viewing point for visitors to see the falls, along with a wide, paved and staired trail that runs through the surrounding park. The path is about a mile long and ends at the foot of the waterfall. Bring a pair of hiking boots or shoes with good tread and a raincoat for light to considerable spray and wet pathways.

Parking

Free public parking is available across the street from Salish Lodge. Please use the footbridge to safely cross over to the lodge.
Valet parking at the lodge costs $8. If you are staying at Salish Lodge, valet parking is complimentary.

Weather & Attire

It will be autumn in the Pacific Northwest; We expect 40-50F temperatures with a likely chance of mist or rain.
We suggest wearing or bringing warm layers and a raincoat or umbrella so you'll be comfortable through to the night.

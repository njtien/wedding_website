To our wonderful friends and family

We have been together for 10 years. A decade! On 11.11, we will be celebrating our 11-year anniversary and wedding.

We grew up on the east coast, lived in the midwest, and recently moved to the west coast. We tried really hard to find a place to have a wedding that would not be difficult for our guests to get to, but in the end, no matter where we chose, the majority if not all of our guests would have to travel by plane or at least car. We decided we might as well run with it and make it a destination wedding.

It'll be quite an adventure and we really hope you can make the trip. We're so happy we'll get a chance to have all of you meet or reunite! We are so excited to see all of you again; it really has been much too long.

Safe travels,
Nancy, Wesley, & Boolean
